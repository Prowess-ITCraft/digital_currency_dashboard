Terms and Conditions
  1. On joining the Partner Program: A welcome activation bonus of 60,000 Digital Coins (hereinafter referred to as DCs) credited to the AP's ITCraft wallet.
  2. Each AP will get their login-based account dashboard showing their DCs & the activation status.
  3. AP is expected to redeem at least 6,000 DCs in a month, else they would lapse on the first day of the next month.
  4. DC Usage:
    • Against on-boarding every SME for PrismSuite Audit → 3,000 DCs are redeemed (only against audit completion).
    • Against 2 audits in the month → 6,000 DCs are redeemed & deducted from the ITCraft wallet.
    • If not redeemed → 6,000 DCs allocated for that month would lapse & hence deducted from the ITCraft wallet.
    • If AP on-boards only one SME for PrismSuite audit in that month → 3,000 DCs redeemed and balance 3,000 DCs get lapsed, effectively deducting 6,000 DCs from the wallet.
    • 6,000 DCs every month will be deducted from the wallet by the first day of next month — if AP uses them for 2 audits: 6,000 DCs are redeemed; against one audit: 3,000 DCs redeemed & 3,000 DCs lapsed; if not used: all 6,000 DCs would lapse.
    • APs using more than 2 audits a month would redeem more DCs accordingly (3,000 DC per audit).
  5. ATP Status Unlock: First 10 APs who fully redeem all 60,000 DCs within 12 months or sooner would automatically earn the agreement to be an 'Exclusive ATP', for yearly recurring earnings.
  6. Earnings:
    • Against redeemed 3,000 DCs per audit; AP earns ₹3,000/- after completion of one month of the audit.
    • If the SME (end customer) decides to go with IT restructuring commercials in that month, AP would get 8.6% of the project value.
    • One-month gap for SME, post audit report submission is for them to take action. If they take action in that month, AP qualifies for 8.6% of the revenue.
    • If SME takes action after a month but within 6 months, the AP would still get 8.6% of the project revenue after deducting ₹3,000/- which has already been paid to them.
    • Either way, AP tends to earn for their efforts — either 8.6% of the project value or ₹3,000/-.
    • ₹3,000/- would be credited into the AP's account after a month and in the AP's login dashboard it will show 3,000 DCs redeemed.
    • If the SME decides to go ahead with the project, post completion & receipt of payments from the SME, AP's account will be credited with 8.6% of the project value.
  7. Milestone Bonus: Complete 5 audits before 31 March 2026 → a total of ₹20,000 (instead of ₹15,000) will be credited into AP's account, only if none of the SMEs move ahead with a project.
  8. Payout Timing: All pay-outs (assured amounts, project revenue share or bonus) are processed 1 month after audit or project completion.
  9. Program Goal is to assist AP to monetize their network & get credited for every effort, however least it could be. Get immediate assured income from audits + long-term bigger project revenue share from project conversions + upgrade to ATP status with YOY income.
    Valid till 31 March 2026.
